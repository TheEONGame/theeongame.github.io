Background

In 2050, the scientists predict that the sun will change from hydrogen fusion to helium fusion in 300 years later. At that time, the sun will be expanded to engulf almost all the planets in the solar system. Human beings must interstellar migration, but the science and technology still cannot travel cross-galaxy.

However, people received a message from who called themselves the "stellar man". They showed their will to help humanity improve technology. But on how to do it, people have different opinions and could not convince the other. So, sadly, a world war broke out.

As a commander of the innovation party, you and your squad will finish the last mission. The Innovation Party demand this victory!
