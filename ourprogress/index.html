Map Design

The game map has four parts: a part of street, a military camp, an L shaped road, and a training ground of the military base. Around them are some mountains. You will get into an alley battle in the first street scene and the final boss will wait you in the last scene.

Scenes Design

To make the scenes look realistic, we add many models in them. You can see burnt cars, empty oil tanks, garbage bins and some other things in the street. They can also be your covers in a bullet rain. There are more in the other scenes which can be discovered by you.

Boss Design

The final boss is a prototype Mecha, which pilot is the leader of enemies. Be careful, commander!
